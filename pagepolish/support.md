# Support for PagePolish

We are here to help you get the most out of PagePolish.

## Contact Support

For bug reports, feature requests, or general assistance, please email us at:

[**support@redkernel.de**](mailto:support@redkernel.de)

## Response Time
We aim to respond to all support inquiries within **2 business days**.

## Reporting Issues
To help us resolve your issue quickly, please include the following information in your email:

1.  **Issue Description**: A clear description of what went wrong or what you were trying to do.
2.  **Screenshots**: If possible, attach screenshots of the error or the formatting issue.
3.  **Browser/OS**: Which browser (e.g., Chrome, Firefox) and operating system (e.g., Windows, Mac) you are using.
4.  **Steps to Reproduce**: Detailed steps so we can continually reproduce the issue.
